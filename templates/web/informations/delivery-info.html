Delivery Informations

Nexsme is an online marketplace facilitation for the local merchants to selling their products/services online. The delivery times/slots available above are to be used only as a guideline and are subject to the acceptance and availability of the products ordered from the respective vendors. For more info, please call +974 66485898 or WhatsApp us at +974 66485898

If any orders contained Fresh Fish and delivery slot fall on Sunday, delivery will be subject to availability of Fresh Fish and Meat from the central market and may be postponed to next day.

Current Lead Time for Order execution is 4 to 6 hours

Current Delivery Offset time is 5 hours (Minimum Time between the ordering and next delivery slot availability)

4 pm is the Cut-off time of the order acceptance/slot availability for same delivery and later received order’s delivery will be scheduled for next day.

We are doing home delivery all over Qatar. Buyer's shipping address, delivery location will be verified with the database of Website before starting the delivery process. In the event Buyer's order is not serviceable (such as inside of restricted industrial plants, military base, oil rigs, access permit required area, etc.) by our logistic service providers or by Seller or the delivery address is not located in an area that is covered under the order confirmation form, Buyer may provide an alternate shipping address on which the Product can be delivered by the logistics service provider or by the Seller.

No any minimum purchase value restrictions on Nexsme!!!
